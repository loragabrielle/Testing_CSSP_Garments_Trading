Our History

With 3 years of manufacturing experience, CSSP has been active since 2024, delivering good quality of clothing all over the world.

2024

2025

2026

Founded in 2024 in Antipolo, Rizal, we began as a small tailoring business dedicating to crafting quality seaman clothing.

By 2025, we steadily grew, earning trust of seafearers through commitment to high-quality and well-crafted garments.

In 2026, we continue to refine our craft, building on our experience to deliver reliable seaman clothing with excellence.

OUR FACTORY

Our place is meticulously organized, clean, and equipped with high-quality materials, allowing our skilled tailors to craft seaman clothing with precision. Every step, from handling fabrics to finishing touches, reflects our commitment to quality.

LOCATION

We are located in Antipolo, Rizal, Philippines, providing well-organized and accesible space that supports efficient production and smooth operations.

SUPPLIER

We source our high-quality fabrics and materials from a trusted Chinese supplier that is based in Binondo, Manila, ensuring that every garment meets our standards.

OWNER

The Pacleb Family leads the business with dedication and expertise, overseeing operations to ensure that quality, craftmanship, and the needs of the seafearers are always a priority.

PROCESS

From selecting the finest fabrics to cutting, sewing, and finishing, every step of our production is carefully managed. Our skilled team ensures that each piece meets our standards of quality and craftmanship.
